Frequently Asked Questions

What does SilentPath actually do?

SilentPath is a silent walking timer and reflection journal. You start a timed walk, the app stays quiet during it (no audio cues, no podcasts, no music), and at the end you jot a short reflection. It is a tool for intentional, distraction-free walks. There is no GPS tracking, no step counting, and no fitness coaching.

Does SilentPath play audio or interrupt my music?

No. SilentPath does not use AVAudioSession and does not play, pause, mix, or duck any audio. If you want a fully silent walk, we recommend enabling Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode in iOS Settings before you start. SilentPath surfaces a one-time tip about this on first launch but does not change your audio settings itself.

What is free and what is paid?

The free tier includes up to 3 walks per week with the reflection journal. Premium unlocks unlimited walks and is offered as a one-time purchase: $3.99 monthly, $19.99 yearly, or $24.99 lifetime. Premium is not required to use the core feature.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

If you chose the monthly or yearly Premium option, you can manage it in iPhone Settings > your name > Subscriptions, find SilentPath, and tap Cancel Subscription. The $24.99 lifetime option is a one-time purchase and has nothing to cancel.

Does SilentPath use Apple Health or HealthKit?

No. SilentPath does not read from or write to HealthKit. It does not access steps, distance, heart rate, or workouts.

Does SilentPath require an account?

No. SilentPath has no account system, no login, and no sign-up. Everything works on-device.

Where is my data stored?

All of your walk history and reflections are stored locally on your device. SilentPath does not upload them to any server. Your data is included in your standard iCloud or iTunes device backups if you have those enabled.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us at cped@outlook.com with a description of the problem

System Requirements

SilentPath requires iOS 17.0 or later.
